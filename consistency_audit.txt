INCONSISTENCIES FOUND
---------------------
1. Animation durations vary: 0.3s, 0.35s, 0.4s, 0.5s
   - Recommend: standardize view transitions to 0.35s

2. Border radius 6px and 10px don't match CSS vars
   - Recommend: use 8px (--border-radius-sm) or 12px (--border-radius)

3. Two different error/delete reds:
   - #ef4444 (changelog negative, modal-change negative)
   - #e74c3c (delete button, status error)
   - Recommend: pick one (suggest #ef4444 as it's used more)

4. Font sizes have many close variants
   - 0.85rem vs 0.9rem (both used for "small text")
   - 1.1rem vs 1.15rem (both used for "medium emphasis")
   - Not critical but could consolidate

5. Unusual padding value 0.875rem
   - Used in admin buttons
   - Consider 0.75rem or 1rem instead
